Ocean Exports


We provide complete logistic and documentary services for a wide range of commodities moving as LCL, container, or break-bulk from the USA to any port worldwide.

Our state-of-the-art software along with our experienced personnel, allows us to negotiate rates, route cargo, arrange transportation, and complete all documentation in order to ship cargo quickly and economically. Upon arrival at destination, the documents are waiting / avoiding delays. Our destination partners are always ready, willing, and able to assist when needed / required.

Based on our years of experience in a very competitive marketplace, we are able to offer a full package of services to ship/sell your products worldwide.
